How I started

It was in 2020, when I got Minecraft for the first time of my life. I watched alot of Minecraft videos to figure out about some things about the game. One random day, an idea popped to my head:
"Why don't I become a YouTuber?"

I talked about it to my father and he was okay with it. I tried some apps for recording and editing but they were - were - not good. So I asked my relatives for some advice on what to use. They told about some good apps to use for recording and editing. I then nailed it down to the perfect two apps. I recorded a couple of videos but never published them. On 21 January 2021, the channels first video was published. I shared it to my family and they loved it. The channels name kept changing; from Yuki Gaming to Yuki Gaming2279 to Yuki Gamer to Yuki Gamer2279 to Glowsquid and then, to the name you all know today, Blue Crafter.
