Introdution

I'm Blue Crafter. A South African YouTuber who posts gaming content and stop-motion.

How I started out

The Blue Crafter channel (first called Yuki Gaming) was founded in 21 January 2021, Thursday, when I published my first video. I shared this video with everyone I knew and carried on publishing videos. I changed the channel name from Yuki Gaming to Yuki Gaming2279 and the Yuki Gamer. After a few months more, I changed the name to Blue Crafter (because there were so many channels named Yuki Gaming etc...). I kept the name and uploaded more videos. I soon made my second channel where I posted coding Arduino and other things.
