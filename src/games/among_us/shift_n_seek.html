Rules of Shift-n-seek

  1. No venting, sabotaging, reporting bodies or calling emergency meetings
  2. No camping
  3. Do tasks
  4. Report cheaters and rule-breakers to the host after the round

    5. Imposter Rules

    1. Imposter(s) must vent at the start of each game
    2. Shift to the person you want to kill
    3. If you killed a crewmate that you didn't shift to, stand on the body for 15 sec

How to play Shift-n-seek?

Shift-n-seek is similar to Hide-n-seek with a few changes. In Shift-n-seek, you still have to hide from seeker(s) (imposter(s)), but for the seeker(s) to seek you, they need to morph into you.
