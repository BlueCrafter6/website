Hide-n-seek

An unofficial Among Us minigame


Rules of Hide-n-seek

  1. No venting, sabotaging, reporting bodies and calling emergency meetings
  2. No camping anywhere on the map
  3. Do tasks
  4. Report cheaters and rule-breakers to the host after the round

    5. Imposter Rules

    1. Imposters must vent at the start of each game

How to setup a Hide-n-seek lobby?

  • Map: Custom
  • Imposters: Custom
  • Emergency:
    • Meetings: 0
    • Cooldown: 0s
  • Discussion Time: 0s
  • Player Speed: 1,5x
  • Vision:
    • Crewmate: 5,0x
    • Imposter: 0,25x
  • Kill:
    • Cooldown: 15,0s
    • Distance: Medium
  • Visual Tasks: ✓
  • Taskbar Updates: Always
  • Tasks:
    • Common: 1
    • Long: 1
    • Short: 2

How to play Hide-n-seek?

Hide-n-seek is a game I'm sure everyone is familiar with from their childhood days. The Among Us version is the altered one.

The aim of the game is to finish all your tasks and hide from the seeker
