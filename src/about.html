How I started

It was 2020 and I was bored and stuck in my house with nothing to do. Then, I was watching some videos on YouTube and idea popped in my mind

"Why don't I become a YouTuber?"

I started off in the spare room of my house with my phone being held up with cupboard pulling things recording a TV with my tablets screen on it. That wasn't working out so I went to my dad for some advice. He told me to use a screen recorder.
Remember:
I knew NOTHING about screen recorders and stuff like that

Then I shot a couple of EXTREMELY BAD videos. Then I asked my uncles for some advice, they told me that I should use an editor. I looked for one and fell in love with it immediatly. I was then ready to publish videos to YouTube (well not yet). I asked my dad if I can publish it and he said yes. I got so excited and went to my tablet to film a video. I hopped onto YouTube Studio and uploaded it there. This was my first video. It got a decent amount of views over the weeks and I uploaded video after video. For my birthday in May, I got an Arduino kit. This was when I made my second channel. Check that story out here.
